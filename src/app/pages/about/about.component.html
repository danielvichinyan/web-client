About Us

'Know It' is a new educational platform which was implemented by Daniel Vichinyan. He is a final-year computer science student at the University of Surrey.
He also works as a Full-Stack Software Developer at Bul Si which is a company located in Bulgaria.
'Know It' offers an innovative and interactive way of teaching and studying. It offers both lectures and quiz competitions to students.
It provides helpful tools for both teachers and students in order to make teaching and studying an enjoyable experience.
